Address

713 Walnut St.
Des Moines, IA
(map)
Location
The Tea Room is located on the 6th floor of the Wilkins building and is accessible by elevator. The street entrance is on 8th street between Walnut and Locust.
Parking
Adjacent to the Tea Room there is Partnership Parking, a parking garage accessible from Locust St. On the corner of 9th and Locust there is another parking garage and you can access the Tea Room by walking through the Skywalk. We're currently looking into whether these parking garages charge on Sundays and will update this website when we find out. Street parking on Sundays is free.
