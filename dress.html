Dress

Expected weather
In the past 6 years, the average temperature was 81 F in the afternoon and 76 F in the evening. The average humidity was 46% in the afternoon and 65% in the evening. There was light rain in the evening on this day three years out of the past six years (50% probability). We recommend dressing for warm weather for the afternoon, and possibly bringing a light jacket and umbrella for the evening.
General
The bride prefers that you wear semi-formal clothing, with the understanding that you may not want to purchase new clothing to wear only once for this event. That said, the minimum level of formality for clothing is business casual, please see below for details.

The bride will be happy help you shop for affordable clothing options that both suit your style and satisfy the dress code for the event. If you have any questions or would like help with shopping for clothing, please contact the bride. Feel free to email her photos of your potential outfit and she will be happy to advise you!
What NOT to wear
Please do not wear jeans, shorts, or T-shirts. In order not to mirror the colors worn by the bride, the groom, and the wedding party, we ask that guests (with the exception of parents of the bride and groom) avoid large blocks of white, ivory, and silver/light gray. If it is convenient, please avoid bold colors in general, like this, unless in small quantities for accent such as a tie, pocket square, floral patterns on clothing, or jewelry.

Women
Business Casual: blouse/button-up shirt with slacks/khakis/skirt.

Semi-Formal: dress, ao dai (Vietnamese traditional dress), pant/skirt suit.
Skirt or dress length can be either long or short.

Color: If possible, the bride prefers that ladies wear light/pastel colors, like this. Dark colors such as black, brown, navy, dark gray, etc, are fine for bottoms (pants, slacks, and skirts) but not for dresses.
Men
Business Casual: A long sleeved button up shirt and long pants/khakis/slacks.
Please do not wear polos or short sleeves.

Semi-Formal: suit or sports jacket.
Ties are not required but certainly welcome.
