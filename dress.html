Dress

Expected weather
In the past 6 years, the average temperature was 81 F in the afternoon and 76 F in the evening. The average humidity was 46% in the afternoon and 65% in the evening. There was light rain in the evening on this day three years out of the past six years (50% probability). We recommend dressing for warm weather for the afternoon, and possibly bringing a light jacket and umbrella for the evening.
Women's dress code
Ladies should wear one of the following: dress, skirt and blouse, skirt suit, dress slacks and blouse, pant suit, or ao dai (Vietnamese traditional dress). The bride prefers that ladies wear pastel colors, like this. Skirt or dress length can be either long or short. In order not to mirror the colors of the wedding dress and the wedding party, we ask that ladies avoid large blocks of white, ivory, and silver. The bride prefers that ladies wear pastel colors, like bride also prefers that ladies additionally avoid large blocks of dark colors and red (like this and this).
Men's dress code
The bride prefers that men wear suits, or a sports jacket with dress slacks. A long sleeved button up shirt and long pants are required. Please do not wear polos, short sleeves, or T-shirts. Khakis are fine but not jeans or shorts. Ties are not required but certainly welcome. Color: In order not to mirror the color of the wedding party and the groom, we ask that men avoid light gray/silver and black.
Questions?
If you have any questions or are unsure about the outfit you have chosen, please contact the bride. Feel free to send her photos of your potential outfit and she will be happy to advise you!
